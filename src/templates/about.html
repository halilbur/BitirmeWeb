About ClothMatch

Advanced AI-powered clothing similarity search using deep learning

How It Works

  1. Upload: Submit your clothing image.
  2. Classify: AI identifies the clothing type among 12 categories.
  3. Analyze: Deep learning extracts style features.
  4. Match: Find similar items in our database.
